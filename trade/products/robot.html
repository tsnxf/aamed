Precision Surgical Robot Arm

Advanced robotic assistance for minimally invasive surgery, featuring haptic feedback and sub-millimeter precision.

Ergonomic Console Design

The surgeon's console is designed for maximum comfort during long procedures, featuring immersive 3D high-definition display and intuitive finger-tip controls that translate hand movements into precise robotic actions.

Multi-Articulated Arms

Four fully articulated robotic arms provide a range of motion greater than the human hand. The compact design allows for easy maneuvering in the operating room and reliable access to the surgical site.
