Customs Documentation

Correctly filed documentation (EORI number, Commercial Invoice, Packing List) is the first step to smooth entry. Our guide details the specific requirements for HS coded machinery to avoid delays at the Port of Hamburg.

CE Marking & Compliance

The CE mark is mandatory for machinery sold in the EEA. It signifies that products are assessed to meet high safety, health, and environmental protection requirements. We explain the self-certification vs. notified body process.
